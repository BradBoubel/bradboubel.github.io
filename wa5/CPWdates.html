2025 Colorado Small Game & Waterfowl

The Small Game Brochure provides hunters with season dates, bag limits, and regulations for waterfowl, upland birds, and other small game animals. While it's an essential reference, the varying rules by species and zone can make it tricky to interpret quickly, so careful review is important before heading into the field.

2025 Season Dates

Here are the key dates for the 2025 Colorado waterfowl hunting seasons:

Northeast Zone

Dates: Oct. 18–Nov. 30; Dec. 11–Jan. 31, 2026
Area: East of I-25 and north of I-70

Southeast Zone

Dates: Oct. 28–Jan. 31, 2026
Area: East of I-25 and south of I-70, and all of El Paso, Pueblo, Huerfano and Las Animas counties.

Mountain/Foothills Zone

Dates: Oct. 4–Nov. 30; Dec. 25–Jan. 31, 2026
Area: West of I-25 and east of the Continental Divide, except El Paso, Pueblo, Huerfano and Las Animas counties

Western Zone

Dates: Oct. 4–21; Nov. 6–Jan. 31, 2026
Area: West of the Continental Divide not included in the Eastern Zone

Eastern Zone

Dates: Oct. 4–Jan. 16, 2026
Area: Routt, Grand, Summit, Eagle and Pitkin counties, those parts of Sa- guache, San Juan, Hinsdale and Mineral counties west of the Continental Divide, and part of Gunnison Co., except the North Fork of the Gunnison River Valley (GMUs 53, 63 and 521), and the part of Moffat Co. east of the northern intersection of CR 29 with the Moffat-Routt Co. line, south along CR 29 to the intersection of CR 29 with the Moffat-Routt Co. line (Elkhead Reservoir State Park)

REMINDER: Be sure to check daily bag limits in the 2025 Small Game Brochure prior to hunting.
