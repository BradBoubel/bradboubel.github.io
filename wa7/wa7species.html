Waterfowl Species of Colorado

The waterfowl shown below represent the species you are most likely to encounter while hunting or observing waterfowl in Colorado. From familiar ducks like Mallards and Pintails, to geese, cranes, and even the American Coot, these species make up the core of Colorado's rich waterfowl community.
