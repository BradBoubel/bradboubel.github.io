Colorado Waterfowl Hunting Starter Checklist

Heres the bottom line up front

We know gear can get expensive, so here’s what you actually need to get started: a hunting license, a shotgun with non-toxic ammo, warm waterproof clothing, waders or boots, and basic field gear like decoys and calls. You don’t need every gadget to enjoy the hunt. Start simple and build your kit as you go.

Hunting License & Stamps:

  • Colorado Small Game License (includes waterfowl)
  • Federal Duck Stamp

Hunting Gear:

  • Shotgun (12 or 20 gauge)
  • Ammunition (non-toxic/steel shot)
  • Waterproof waders or boots
  • Camouflage clothing (weather appropriate)
  • Hunting vest or pack

Field Equipment:

  • Decoys
  • Duck/goose calls
  • Blinds or camouflage gear
  • Decoy anchors/weights

Safety & Navigation:

  • Map or GPS/OnX
  • Cell phone or communication device
  • First aid kit

Other Essentials:

  • Game bags or cooler for harvested birds
  • License/permit holder
  • Snacks/water for long days in the field
